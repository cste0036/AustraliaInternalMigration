Internal Migration of Australians Dashboard

The year displayed can be adjusted using the slider.


Data provided by the ABS.

The Australian migration dashboard provides information of the movements of Australian people over the last 20 years.

As a highly mobile nationality with some of the highest internal migration in the world. Monitoring these movements and the socio-economic factors that affect them is important to understanding the Australian people.

Red markers mean a negative net migration.

Green markers mean a positive net migration.

How to Use.

Clicking on a state will provide additional information on the distribution of employment amongst commercial industries for that state.

each element can be hovered to provide the numeric value.
